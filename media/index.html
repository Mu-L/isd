Referencing isd in the media

If you are writing about the isd project, plase make sure to use lower-case letters (isd and not ISD). isd stands for interactive systemd and since systemd is written in lower-case it makes sense to also write isd in lower-case.

The prononciation are the literal letters i-s-d (eye-es-de).

Thank you for reading this and respecting my wishes! 🙏
